About WorldHub

WorldHub is a professional social network built around specialized communities rather than a single, undifferentiated feed. Every "World" — Programming, Medicine, Design, Entrepreneurship and more — has its own posts, marketplace, jobs board, voice rooms and AI assistant tuned to that field.

We started WorldHub because general-purpose social networks dilute professional conversation with noise. By organizing the platform around fields of interest, members find higher-signal discussions, more relevant job opportunities, and a marketplace of tools actually built for their craft.

Our Mission

To give every profession and passion its own thriving, focused corner of the internet — with the tools members actually need: content, commerce, hiring, and real-time collaboration, all in one place.

What we value

Relevance

Communities organized by field, not algorithm-chasing engagement.

Trust & Safety

Verification and reporting tools to keep communities credible.

Craft

Tools built for the specific needs of each profession.

The team

WorldHub is built by a small, distributed team of engineers, designers and community builders who believe the future of professional networking is specialized, not generic.

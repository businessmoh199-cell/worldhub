Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WorldHub ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, profile details you provide.
  • Content you create: posts, comments, messages, marketplace listings.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, device and browser information.
  • Communications: messages exchanged through chat, voice rooms, and the AI assistant.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to operate and improve WorldHub, personalize your feed and recommendations, facilitate communication between members, process marketplace transactions and subscriptions, and maintain platform safety and integrity.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the platform (hosting, payment processing, analytics), or when required by law.

4. Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your profile information at any time from your account settings. You may request a copy of your data or full account deletion by contacting support@worldhub.app.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data, including encryption in transit and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, and we encourage strong, unique passwords.

6. Children's Privacy

WorldHub is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the platform or via email.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@worldhub.app.
